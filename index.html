Africa is Sparking.

Provide real insights into Africa.

Founded in 2017, we aim to solve the problem that Japanese people have few interests in Africa. To break through the status quo, we provide real insights to Africa and build the community in Japan through following approaches.

What We Do

Research

We research topics mainly in Sub-Saharan Africa. Through our own media and social media, we will update the latest information about Africa.

Event

By hosting events, we make an offline community where those who are interested in Africa can connect each other and exchange their insights.

Design

We design our unique projects collaborating with many stakeholders. So far, we have carried out field researches in African countries.

Areas of Focus

We will focus on culture and technology scene in Africa.

Africa is a huge continent. It contains 54 independent countries. The population is over 1.2 billion. Africa is vibrant. Every each country has unique culture.
